Our Mission

Our mission is to provide vital support to communities across Louisiana as they navigate the challenges of recovering from natural disasters and economic hardship. Through collaborative efforts, resource-sharing, and unwavering dedication, we strive to foster resilience, rebuild lives, and create a brighter future for all. Join us in making a meaningful and lasting impact as we work together to strengthen our communities and bring hope to those in need.

About Revive

The Revive Louisiana Volunteer Project is dedicated to helping communities across the state of Louisiana recover from the devastation caused by natural disasters. ReviveLA, as it is affectionally known, was birthed out of tragedy, and resilience, during the 2016 Great Flood that affected most of Louisiana. Based in Baton Rouge, LA, our organization provides support to residents affected by hurricanes, flooding, tornadoes, and other catastrophic events. Though we are located in Baton Rouge, we deploy volunteers across all affected parishes during times of crisis, working tirelessly to rebuild, restore, and provide essential resources.

Our mission is driven by the collective efforts of passionate individuals who come together to support their neighbors.
